Kedarnath Temple is a Hindu temple, one of the twelve jyotirlinga of Shiva.
One of the most interesting features of the Kedarnath Temple is its unique lingam.
The temple is located on the Garhwal Himalayan range near the Mandakini river, in the state of Uttarakhand, India.
Unlike other lingams that are made of stone or metal, people believe it to be a natural rock formation in the shape of a lingam.
